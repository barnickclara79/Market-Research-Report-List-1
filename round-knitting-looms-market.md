<p><p>Round knitting looms are specialized tools used for creating knitted fabric in a circular shape, typically used in crafting hats, scarves, and various garments. The convenience and versatility of round knitting looms have made them popular among both novice and experienced knitters.</p><p>The Round Knitting Looms Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.88% during the forecast period, driven by a rising interest in DIY craft projects and the growing popularity of knitting as a therapeutic activity.
