<p><ul><li>Reflective Displays</li><li>Transmissive Displays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-display market is primarily divided into two types: reflective displays and transmissive displays. Reflective displays utilize ambient light to illuminate the image, making them energy-efficient and ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, transmissive displays rely on a backlight to shine through the display, offering vibrant colors and sharp images, making them suitable for indoor use. Each type caters to specific needs, with reflective displays excelling in visibility and energy savings, while transmissive displays focus on image quality and color accuracy.</p></p>
