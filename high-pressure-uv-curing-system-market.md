<p><p>The High Pressure UV Curing System market is segmented into three main types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure systems are designed for localized curing, providing precise application of UV light to specific areas. Flood Cure systems, on the other hand, expose larger surfaces to uniform UV light, ideal for extensive applications. Focused Beam systems utilize concentrated UV light to achieve deeper penetration and faster curing times, making them suitable for complex geometries and materials requiring thorough curing.</p></p>
