<p><strong>The Polylactams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrolytic Polymerization</li><li>Anion Polymerization</li><li>Positive Ion Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polylactams market is segmented based on different polymerization methods. Hydrolytic polymerization involves the reaction of lactams with water, leading to the formation of polyamides. Anion polymerization utilizes negatively charged ions to initiate polymerization, allowing for more control over the molecular weight and structure of the resulting polymer. Positive ion polymerization, on the other hand, employs positively charged ions to facilitate the polymerization process, often resulting in different properties. Each method offers unique advantages influencing their application in various industries.</p></p>
