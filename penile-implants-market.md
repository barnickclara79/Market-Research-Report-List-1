<p><p>The penile implants market features three primary types: three-piece inflatable, two-piece inflatable, and semirigid rods. The three-piece inflatable implant consists of two cylinders, a pump, and a fluid reservoir, allowing for adjustable rigidity and natural appearance. The two-piece inflatable variant simplifies installation with fewer components, providing satisfactory rigidity but less control. Semirigid rods offer a fixed option, maintaining a constant state of rigidity, ideal for patients seeking a straightforward solution without requiring mechanical adjustments. Each type caters to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
