<p><p>The cardiac ablation catheters market is primarily segmented into multi-electrode and single-point ablation catheters. Multi-electrode ablation catheters feature multiple electrodes that can deliver simultaneous energy, enabling more efficient and broader tissue ablation, suitable for complex arrhythmias. In contrast, single-point ablation catheters focus on delivering energy at a single site, offering precision in targeted areas, ideal for simpler arrhythmias. Both types are critical for improving patient outcomes in the treatment of various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
