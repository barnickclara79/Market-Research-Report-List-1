<p><p>Hybrid cars and electric vehicles (EVs) encompass three main market types: HEVs (Hybrid Electric Vehicles), PHEVs (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles), and EVs (Battery Electric Vehicles). HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, enhancing fuel efficiency without needing external charging. PHEVs also utilize both power sources but can be charged via an electric outlet, allowing for extended electric-only driving ranges. EVs rely solely on batteries for propulsion, offering zero emissions and reduced operating costs, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
