<p><strong>The Phosphate Bronze Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CuSn5</li><li>CuSn6</li><li>CuSn8</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phosphate bronze wire encompasses various grades, including CuSn5, CuSn6, and CuSn8, each characterized by different copper and tin compositions. CuSn5 contains approximately 5% tin, offering good wear resistance and moderate strength, making it suitable for electrical applications. CuSn6, with around 6% tin, provides enhanced strength and is often used in demanding industrial settings. CuSn8, containing about 8% tin, delivers superior mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, making it ideal for applications like springs and connectors where durability is critical.</p></p>
