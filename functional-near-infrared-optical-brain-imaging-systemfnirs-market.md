The fNIRS market includes various types, such as FT-NIR Spectroscopy, which utilizes Fourier Transform methods for detailed spectral analysis; Dispersive NIR Spectroscopy, which separates light into its component wavelengths; and other technologies like Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTF) and filter-based systems, which offer flexibility in wavelength selection for specific applications. These methods enhance brain imaging capabilities in research and clinical settings.</p></p>
