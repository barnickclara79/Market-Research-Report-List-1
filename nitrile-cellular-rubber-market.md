<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Rubber</li><li>Semi-closed Cell Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrile Cellular Rubber is categorized into two main types: closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber. Closed cell rubber features a compact structure, preventing liquid absorption and offering excellent thermal insulation and buoyancy, making it suitable for seals and gaskets. In contrast, semi-closed cell rubber has a less dense structure, allowing some absorption while still providing good insulation properties. This type is often used in applications where flexibility and conformability are essential, such as in cushioning and padding materials.</p></p>
