<p><p>The truck steel wheel market can be categorized into three main types: casting, forging, and others. </p><p>Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds to create wheels with complex shapes, allowing for mass production. Forging, on the other hand, uses heat and pressure to shape solid steel, resulting in stronger and more durable wheels. The "other" segment includes alternative manufacturing methods, such as machining or hybrid processes, which may combine characteristics of casting and forging to meet specific performance or cost requirements.</p></p>
