<p><strong>The Medical Laminated Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical laminated tubes are essential for packaging pharmaceutical products, and they typically come in two types: ABL (Aluminium Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes offer superior protection against external factors like moisture and light due to their aluminum content, making them ideal for sensitive formulations. In contrast, PBL tubes, made primarily from plastics, are lightweight and flexible, providing adequate barrier properties for many applications, though typically less than ABL. Each type serves specific needs in the medical packaging sector.</p></p>
