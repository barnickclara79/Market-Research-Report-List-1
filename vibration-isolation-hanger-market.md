<p><p>The vibration isolation hanger market comprises several types, including spring, rubber, fiberglass, and others. Rubber hangers use elastomeric materials to minimize noise and vibration transfer, ideal for light to medium applications. Fiberglass hangers offer high durability and resistance to environmental factors, suitable for harsh conditions. Other types may include specialized materials or designs tailored to specific industrial needs, enhancing vibration control across various sectors.</p></p>
