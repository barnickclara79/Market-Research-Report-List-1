<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine power cables market primarily consists of single core and multi-core cable types. Single core cables contain one conductor, making them suitable for high voltage applications over long distances. They are often used in point-to-point connections. Multi-core cables, on the other hand, feature multiple conductors within a single cable sheath, enhancing flexibility and simplifying installation for land and offshore wind farms. Both types play crucial roles in renewable energy sourcing and interconnection between energy grids.</p></p>
