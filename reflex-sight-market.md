<p><p>The reflex sight market is primarily categorized into two types: cylindrical and window. Cylindrical reflex sights feature a rounded design, enhancing durability and offering a compact profile, which is favored for tactical applications. In contrast, window reflex sights provide a larger field of view, allowing for quicker target acquisition and improved situational awareness. Both types are widely used in firearms, airsoft, and archery, catering to different user preferences based on accuracy, speed, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
