<p><p>The Aerospace Life Jacket market comprises three main types: foam jackets, inflatable jackets, and hybrid jackets. Foam jackets are made of buoyant materials, offering reliable flotation without inflation. Inflatable jackets are compact and lightweight, expanding to provide buoyancy upon activation, making them ideal for aircraft due to space-saving qualities. Hybrid jackets combine features of both foam and inflatable designs, offering versatility and enhanced safety. Each type serves specific needs in emergency situations, emphasizing comfort and reliability for aircraft passengers and crew.</p></p>
