<p><ul><li>Solid Fiber</li><li>Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slip sheet market primarily comprises two types: solid fiber and plastic slip sheets. Solid fiber slip sheets are made from compressed paper materials, offering strength and eco-friendliness, making them suitable for various industries. In contrast, plastic slip sheets are constructed from high-density polyethylene or polypropylene, providing durability and moisture resistance. Both types serve as a lightweight alternative to wooden pallets, enhancing efficiency in transportation and storage, reducing shipping costs, and minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
