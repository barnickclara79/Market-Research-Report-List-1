<p><p>Capsule filling and sealing machines are categorized into three main types based on operational efficiency. Manual capsule filling machines require human intervention for the filling process, making them suitable for small-scale production. Semi-automatic machines offer a balance, where some functions are automated, enhancing efficiency while still needing operator input. Fully automatic capsule filling machines operate independently, maximizing throughput and minimizing labor costs, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing and high-demand environments. Each type serves different production needs and capacities.</p></p>
