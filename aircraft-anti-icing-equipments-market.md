<p><p>The Aircraft Anti-Icing Equipment market includes several technologies designed to prevent ice formation on aircraft surfaces. Electric Pulse Anti-Icing Systems use electric impulses to dislodge ice, while Liquid Anti-Icing Systems spray de-icing fluids to melt ice. Hot Air Anti-Icing Systems circulate heated air around critical components to prevent freezing, whereas Electric Heating Anti-Icing Systems utilize resistive heating elements to warm surfaces directly. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and performance in icy conditions during flight.</p></p>
