<p><p>The capacitive accelerometer sensors market is segmented into 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis types based on the number of measurement axes. 1-axis sensors detect motion along a single direction, making them suitable for applications like tilt detection. 2-axis sensors measure motion across two dimensions, ideal for devices requiring more spatial awareness, such as gaming controllers. 3-axis sensors provide comprehensive motion sensing in all three dimensions, commonly used in smartphones, wearables, and automotive applications for enhanced navigation and stability analysis.</p></p>
