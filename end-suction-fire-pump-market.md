<p><p>The End Suction Fire Pump market is categorized into two main types: Engine Driven and Electric Driven pumps. Engine Driven pumps utilize an internal combustion engine, making them ideal for remote locations or scenarios where electrical power is unavailable. They offer high mobility and can operate in challenging environments. In contrast, Electric Driven pumps rely on electric power sources, providing consistent performance and lower operational costs in urban settings with reliable electricity. Both types are essential for efficient fire protection systems.</p></p>
