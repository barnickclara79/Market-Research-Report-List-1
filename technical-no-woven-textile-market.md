<p><p>The technical non-woven textile market comprises several types, including natural fibers, synthetic polymers, regenerated fibers, mineral fibers, metal fibers, and specialty fibers. Natural fibers, derived from plants or animals, offer biodegradability and sustainability. Synthetic polymers, like polypropylene and polyester, provide durability and versatility. Regenerated fibers, such as viscose and lyocell, are made from natural materials but processed chemically. Mineral fibers, like fiberglass, are heat-resistant. Metal fibers enhance conductivity, while specialty fibers cater to niche applications, promoting innovation and performance in various industries.</p></p>
