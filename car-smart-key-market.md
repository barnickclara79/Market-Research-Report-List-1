<p><strong>The Car Smart Key Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Smart Key market includes two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicle without physically using the key, as the system detects the key fob's proximity. RKES requires manual interaction, such as pressing a button on the key fob to lock, unlock, or start the car. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more seamless, hands-free experience compared to RKES.</p></p>
