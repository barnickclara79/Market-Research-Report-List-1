<p><ul><li>Manual (Single-Venturi System)</li><li>Automatic (Multi-Venturi System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Constant Volume Sampler (CVS) market includes two main types: Manual and Automatic systems. The Manual system, utilizing a Single-Venturi design, requires operator involvement to gather exhaust samples, making it suitable for smaller applications or limited budgets. In contrast, the Automatic system employs a Multi-Venturi design, allowing for continuous and precise sampling without manual intervention, ideal for larger, more complex installations. Both systems are essential for accurately measuring emissions from combustion engines in compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
