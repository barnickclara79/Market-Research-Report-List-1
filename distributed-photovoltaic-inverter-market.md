<p><strong>The Distributed Photovoltaic Inverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The distributed photovoltaic inverter market comprises three main types: grid-tied, grid/hybrid, and off-grid systems. Grid-tied inverters connect directly to the utility grid, allowing surplus energy to be fed back for savings. Grid/hybrid systems can operate with or without the grid, using batteries to store energy for backup. Off-grid inverters function independently from the grid, providing power in remote areas by directly converting solar energy for local consumption, making them essential for self-sufficient energy solutions.</p></p>
