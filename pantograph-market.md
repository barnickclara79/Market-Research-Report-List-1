<p><p>The Pantograph market is categorized into three main types: single-arm, double-arm, and others. Single-arm pantographs are typically lightweight and used for low-speed trains, allowing easier maneuverability. Double-arm pantographs offer greater stability and are commonly found in high-speed rail systems, enhancing contact reliability with overhead wires. The "others" category includes specialized designs like retractable or hybrid systems, tailored for specific operational needs or unique railway environments, ensuring diverse applications across various transportation sectors.</p></p>
