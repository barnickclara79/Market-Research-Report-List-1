<p><p>The Automotive Active Rear Axle Steering market includes two main types: Single Actuator and Dual Actuator systems. Single Actuator systems use one motor to control rear-wheel steering, offering improved maneuverability and stability at lower costs. Dual Actuator systems employ two motors, enabling independent control of each rear wheel for enhanced precision, agility, and dynamic handling, especially in high-performance or luxury vehicles. Both systems aim to optimize vehicle performance, with Dual Actuator systems providing advanced capabilities at a higher cost.</p></p>
