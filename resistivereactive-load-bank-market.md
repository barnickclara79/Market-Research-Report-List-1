<p><p>A resistive/reactive load bank is a device used to test and simulate electrical loads for power generation systems. It helps ensure that generators and other power sources can handle the required load under various conditions. These load banks can provide resistive load (generating heat) or reactive load (inducing reactive power), making them essential for evaluating the performance and reliability of power systems.</p><p>The resistive/reactive load bank market is experiencing significant growth, fueled by the increasing demand for reliable power sources and the rising investment in infrastructure development.
