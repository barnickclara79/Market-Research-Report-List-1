<p><p>The smoke extractor exhaust fan market is categorized into three main types: centrifugal, axial, and others. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to create high-pressure airflow, making them effective for long duct runs and high resistance systems. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan shaft, providing a direct airflow path, and are ideal for short, straight ducts. Other types may include mixed flow or specialized fans designed for specific applications, enhancing overall ventilation and smoke extraction efficiency.</p></p>
