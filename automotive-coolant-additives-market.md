<p><p>The automotive coolant additives market includes several types, each serving specific purposes in engine cooling systems. Organic Acid Automotive Coolant Additives (OAT) offer extended life and corrosion protection without silicates. Phosphate Automotive Coolant Additives enhance thermal stability and prevent rust, while Silicate Automotive Coolant Additives provide a protective layer against corrosion but have a shorter lifespan. Amine Automotive Coolant Additives prevent oxidation and promote corrosion resistance. Each type caters to different engine requirements and manufacturer specifications.</p></p>
