<p><p>The 2-phase stepper motors market features two primary types: bipolar and unipolar. Bipolar stepper motors utilize two winding phases and require a more complex driving circuit, offering higher torque and efficiency in applications like robotics and CNC machines. In contrast, unipolar motors have a simpler design with one winding per phase and are easier to control, making them suitable for low-power applications such as printers and small automation tasks. Both types cater to distinct user needs and preferences within the market.</p></p>
