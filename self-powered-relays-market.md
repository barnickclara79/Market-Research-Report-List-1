<p><strong>The Self Powered Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inverse Time Over Current Relays</li><li>Definite Time Over Current Relays</li><li>Instantaneous Current Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-powered relays market consists of three primary types: Inverse Time Over Current Relays, Definite Time Over Current Relays, and Instantaneous Current Relays. Inverse Time Over Current Relays respond more slowly to lower overcurrents, providing protection by delaying operation based on severity. Definite Time Over Current Relays operate after a preset time, regardless of current magnitude, ensuring consistent protection. Instantaneous Current Relays act immediately upon detecting an overcurrent, delivering rapid fault isolation to safeguard electrical systems. Each type serves specific protective functions in power management.</p></p>
