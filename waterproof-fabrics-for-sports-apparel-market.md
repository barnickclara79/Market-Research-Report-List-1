<p><ul><li>Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li><li>Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports apparel market features two main types of waterproof fabrics: breathable and non-breathable. Breathable waterproof fabrics allow moisture and sweat to escape while preventing water from getting in, making them ideal for high-intensity activities where ventilation is crucial. In contrast, non-breathable waterproof fabrics provide a barrier against water but can trap heat and moisture, making them suitable for less vigorous pursuits or conditions requiring maximum water resistance. Each type caters to specific performance needs and environmental conditions in sports.</p></p>
