<p><p>Brachytherapy afterloaders are specialized devices used to deliver localized radiation treatment, primarily for cancer. The High Dose Rate (HDR) afterloaders administer a high dose of radiation in a short duration, maximizing tumor targeting while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissues. Conversely, Pulsed Dose Rate (PDR) afterloaders deliver radiation in a series of pulses, allowing for a more prolonged therapeutic effect over time. Both types enhance the precision and effectiveness of brachytherapy, catering to varying treatment needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
