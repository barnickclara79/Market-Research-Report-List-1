<p><p>The additives for inkjet inks market encompasses various types that enhance ink performance. Humectants and co-solvents improve ink flow and prevent drying, while surfactants enhance wetting and adhesion to substrates. Defoamers reduce air bubbles that can affect print quality, ensuring a smoother application. Rheology modifiers adjust viscosity for optimal ink behavior during printing, promoting consistency. Other additives may include biocides and stabilizers that enhance shelf life and prevent microbial growth, supporting overall ink performance and durability.</p></p>
