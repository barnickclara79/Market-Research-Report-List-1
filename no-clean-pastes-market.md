<p><p>The no-clean pastes market is divided into two primary types: lead-containing and lead-free pastes. Lead-containing pastes, traditionally used in electronics, provide excellent soldering properties but are increasingly restricted due to health and environmental concerns. In contrast, lead-free pastes have gained popularity, particularly with regulatory changes promoting safer manufacturing practices. Lead-free options utilize alternative metals like silver, tin, and copper, offering comparable performance while adhering to global standards for sustainability and safety in electronics production.</p></p>
