<p><strong>The Biohazard Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type A</li><li>Type B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biohazard hoods, also known as biosafety cabinets, are categorized into Type A and Type B based on their design and application. Type A hoods are primarily used for handling non-volatile materials and provide personnel and environmental protection through HEPA filtration, recirculating air within the cabinet. Type B hoods, on the other hand, are designed for working with volatile substances and exhaust air directly to the outside, ensuring both personnel safety and preventing contamination. Each type serves specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
