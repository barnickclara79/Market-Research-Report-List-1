Soft rolling covers are made from flexible materials, allowing for easy installation and access while providing moderate protection against the elements. Retractable tonneau covers feature a mechanism that allows the cover to slide back smoothly, offering a blend of convenience and security while maintaining a sleek profile. Each type caters to specific user needs.</p></p>
