<p><p>The UV Grade Fused Silica Lenses market is divided primarily into convex and concave lenses. Convex lenses are outward-curving and focus light to a point, making them essential for applications such as laser systems and imaging technologies. In contrast, concave lenses curve inward and diverge light rays, which helps in correcting optical aberrations or expanding light beams. Both types are crucial in industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, and scientific research, where precision optics are necessary for effective performance.</p></p>
