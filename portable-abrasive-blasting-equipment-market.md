<p><strong>The Portable Abrasive Blasting Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure</li><li>Siphon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable abrasive blasting equipment is categorized primarily into pressure and siphon systems. Pressure blasters utilize compressed air to propel abrasive materials at high speeds, enabling efficient and powerful cleaning or surface preparation. In contrast, siphon blasters rely on a vacuum created by air flowing through a nozzle to draw abrasive material from a container, providing a more economical option but generally delivering lower performance. Both types serve various industries, including construction, automotive, and manufacturing, for effective surface treatment.</p></p>
