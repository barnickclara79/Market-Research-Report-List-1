<p><p>The HVAC refrigerant market comprises three main types: fluorocarbons, hydrocarbons, and inorganic refrigerants. Fluorocarbons, like HFCs and HCFCs, are widely used for their efficiency but face environmental concerns due to their global warming potential. Hydrocarbons, such as propane and isobutane, are more environmentally friendly and offer high energy efficiency but are flammable. Inorganic refrigerants, including ammonia and water, are often used in industrial applications due to their low environmental impact and high performance, though they may have safety considerations.</p></p>
