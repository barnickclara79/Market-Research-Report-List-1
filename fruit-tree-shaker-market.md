<p><p>The fruit tree shaker market comprises three main types: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted shakers are attached to tractors, offering flexibility and ease of use in various orchards. Self-propelled shakers are independent machines equipped with their own engines, providing greater mobility and efficiency in harvesting. Trailed shakers follow behind a vehicle, allowing for the transportation of equipment while maintaining a strong connection to the pulling machine. Each type caters to different operational needs and orchard sizes.</p></p>
