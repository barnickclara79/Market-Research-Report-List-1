<p><p>The pregnancy and fertility testing kits market includes several types, each serving distinct purposes. LH urine tests detect the surge in luteinizing hormone, signaling ovulation. FSH urine tests measure follicle-stimulating hormone levels to assess ovarian function. hCG blood tests quantify human chorionic gonadotropin in blood, confirming pregnancy with high accuracy. hCG urine tests similarly detect hCG but in urine, providing a convenient home testing option. These types cater to various needs, from conception tracking to early pregnancy confirmation.</p></p>
