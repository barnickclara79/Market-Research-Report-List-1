<p><strong>The Cleanroom HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Decentralized</li><li>Centralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cleanroom HVAC market is primarily divided into decentralized and centralized systems. Decentralized systems rely on individual units for temperature and airflow control, providing flexibility and localized management, ideal for smaller cleanrooms or modular applications. In contrast, centralized systems use a network of ducts and a central unit to maintain climate control, offering efficiency and consistency across larger cleanroom operations. The choice between these systems depends on the specific operational requirements and spatial constraints of the cleanroom environment.</p></p>
