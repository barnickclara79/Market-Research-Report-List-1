<p><p>Steckel mills are specialized rolling mills used in the production of hot-rolled steel products. In a single stand market, the mill processes material in one pass, making it suitable for lighter gauge steel and reduced operational complexity. Conversely, a twin stand market involves two stands working in tandem, enabling higher production capacities and greater thickness ranges. This configuration is advantageous for heavier gauges and more complex specifications, providing increased efficiency and flexibility in steel production.</p></p>
