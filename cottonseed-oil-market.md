<p><p>The cottonseed oil market comprises two primary types: crude cottonseed oil and edible cottonseed oil. Crude cottonseed oil is extracted from cotton seeds and serves as a raw ingredient for further refinement. It is often used in industrial applications and for manufacturing products such as soaps and cosmetics. Edible cottonseed oil, on the other hand, undergoes refining to meet food safety standards and is primarily used for culinary purposes, including frying and salad dressings, catering to consumer health preferences.</p></p>
