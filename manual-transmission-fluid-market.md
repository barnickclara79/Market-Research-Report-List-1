<p><p>The Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF) market comprises three main types: Mineral MTF, Synthetic MTF, and Semi-Synthetic MTF. Mineral MTF is derived from crude oil, offering basic lubrication and cost-effectiveness but limited performance under extreme conditions. Synthetic MTF is chemically engineered for superior thermal stability, durability, and efficiency, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Semi-Synthetic MTF blends mineral and synthetic bases, balancing cost and enhanced performance, making it suitable for moderate driving conditions. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs and operating environments.</p></p>
