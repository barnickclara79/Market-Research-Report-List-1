<p><strong>The Lychee Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracted Honey</li><li>Pressed Honey</li><li>Comb Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lychee honey market features three main types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is processed by removing honey from the honeycomb using heat or centrifugal force, resulting in a clear, liquid product. Pressed honey is obtained by crushing the honeycomb and allowing the honey to flow out, retaining some wax particles. Comb honey involves the sale of whole honeycomb, which contains honey sealed in beeswax cells, offering a natural and aesthetically pleasing option with a unique texture.</p></p>
