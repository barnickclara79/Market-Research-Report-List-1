<p><strong>The Horizontal Roller Mills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Rollers</li><li>Four Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal roller mills are categorized into two main types based on the number of rollers: double rollers and four rollers. Double roller mills utilize two horizontal rollers to crush and grind materials, offering efficient processing for smaller production needs. In contrast, four roller mills consist of an additional pair of rollers, enhancing throughput and fineness of the grind, making them ideal for larger-scale industrial applications. Both types are popular in the milling sector for their effectiveness in particle size reduction.</p></p>
